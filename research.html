Geometry Field Splatting with Gaussian Surfels

CVPR 2025 (Oral)
We introduce a novel approach for 3D geometry representation using Gaussian surfels, enabling efficient and high-quality surface reconstruction from point clouds. Our method leverages the flexibility of Gaussian splatting while maintaining geometric accuracy for complex surface structures.
Kaiwen Jiang / Venkataram Sivaram / Cheng Peng / Ravi Ramamoorthi

Neural Geometry Fields for Meshes

SIGGRAPH 2024 (Conference)
We introduce Neural Geometry Fields (NGFs), a novel representation that encodes mesh geometry as a continuous field parameterized by a neural network. NGFs enable efficient mesh editing, interpolation, and generation while maintaining geometric accuracy and supporting standard mesh operations.
Venkataram Sivaram / Tzu-Mao Li / Ravi Ramamoorthi

Parameter-space ReSTIR for Differentiable and Inverse Rendering

SIGGRAPH 2023 (Conference)
An extension of ReSTIR (Reservoir-based Spatio-Temporal Importance Resampling) to parameter space, enabling efficient gradient computation for differentiable rendering and inverse rendering applications. This work bridges the gap between traditional rendering and modern differentiable approaches.
Wesley Chang / Venkataram Sivaram / Derek Nowrouzezahrai / Toshiya Hachisuka / Ravi Ramamoorthi / Tzu-Mao Li
